## Overview

Finding clients is the first challenge for any voice AI agency. This guide helps you choose the right lead generation strategies for your niche and stage. The best strategy depends on where you are in your journey -- starting out operators should focus on high-conversion, low-volume strategies (referrals, warm outreach) before scaling to higher-volume approaches.

There is no single "best" lead gen strategy. The operators who grow fastest combine 2-3 approaches that match their stage, personality, and niche. This guide walks through each strategy with actionable steps so you can start generating leads this week.

## Strategy Selection

Use this framework to choose which strategies to prioritize. Strategies are ordered by conversion rate -- start with the highest-converting approaches and add volume as you grow.

| Strategy | Best For | Volume | Conversion Rate | Effort | Cost |
|----------|----------|--------|-----------------|--------|------|
| Referrals | All stages | Low (1-3/mo) | Very High (30-50%) | Low | Free |
| Partnerships | Getting traction+ | Medium (5-15/mo) | High (20-30%) | Medium | Free |
| LinkedIn Outreach | All stages | Medium (10-30/mo) | Medium (5-15%) | High | Free/Low |
| Content Marketing | Getting traction+ | High (20-50/mo) | Low (2-5%) | High | Low |
| Networking/Events | All stages | Low (2-5/mo) | High (15-25%) | Medium | Low-Medium |
| Cold Email | Getting traction+ | High (50-200/mo) | Low (1-3%) | Medium | Low |

### Stage-Gated Recommendations

**Starting out (0 clients):** Referrals + LinkedIn Outreach. Your ONLY goal is closing your first client. Use high-conversion strategies. Do NOT try to scale yet. One client with great results is worth more than 100 cold leads.

**Getting traction (1-3 clients):** Add Partnerships + Content Marketing. You have proof of concept now. Leverage case studies from your first clients. Start building relationships with complementary service providers who serve {{target_niche}}.

**Scaling (5+ clients):** All strategies active. Optimize based on what is working. Consider adding cold email at volume. Track conversion rates across channels and double down on your top performers.

## Strategy 1: Referrals

The highest-converting lead source for voice AI agencies. Every satisfied client should be asked for referrals -- most operators skip this entirely.

### When to Ask

- After the first month of successful deployment (agent is live, handling calls, client is happy)
- After sharing positive results (e.g., "You answered 47 calls last week that would have gone to voicemail")
- After collecting a testimonial
- During quarterly business reviews

### How to Ask

Keep it natural and specific. Generic "know anyone?" questions get generic responses.

**Template:** "Do you know any other {{target_niche}} owners who struggle with [missed calls / slow lead response / after-hours inquiries]? I helped you [specific result], and I'd love to do the same for someone you know."

**Follow-up if they say yes:** "Would you be comfortable making a quick introduction via text or email? I'll take it from there -- no pressure on them or you."

### Referral Incentives

| Incentive | When to Use | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| One month free service | Best for early-stage agencies | Simple, high perceived value |
| Reduced monthly rate | Best for ongoing relationships | 10-15% discount for each active referral |
| Gift card ($50-100) | When client might not want a service credit | Starbucks, Amazon, or restaurant gift cards |
| Feature upgrade | Best when you have tiered pricing | Add SMS follow-up, additional phone line, etc. |

### Tracking

- Log each referral source in your outreach files (`context/outreach/`)
- Track which clients generate the most referrals -- nurture those relationships
- Goal: 1-2 referral introductions per active client per quarter
- Use `/agency-ops:outreach` to track referral follow-ups alongside other channels

## Strategy 2: Partnerships

Partner with complementary service providers who already serve {{target_niche}} businesses. They have the relationships -- you have the solution they cannot offer.

### Who to Partner With

Find providers whose services complement (not compete with) voice AI:

| Niche | High-Value Partner Types |
|-------|------------------------|
| Dental | Dental marketing agencies, practice management consultants, Dentrix/Eaglesoft integrators |
| Med Spa | Aesthetic marketing agencies, patient management software vendors, injection trainers |
| Law Firms | Legal marketing agencies, practice management consultants, virtual receptionist services |
| HVAC/Home Services | Home service marketing agencies, ServiceTitan consultants, dispatch software vendors |
| Real Estate | Real estate coaches, brokerage technology consultants, lead gen platform reps |

### Partnership Models

**Referral fee (most common):** Pay 10-20% of first month's revenue for each referred client. Simple, low-risk for both sides.

**Co-marketing:** Joint webinar, shared case study, or co-authored blog post. Works well with marketing agencies who need content.

**White-label:** Partner sells your service under their brand. Higher volume but lower margins. Only pursue this at the scaling stage.

### How to Pitch a Partnership

**Opening:** "I help your {{target_niche}} clients capture more revenue from their existing phone traffic and web leads using AI voice agents."

**Value prop:** "When your clients miss calls or respond slowly to web inquiries, it undermines the marketing you do for them. I solve that problem, which makes your services more effective."

**Ask:** "Would you be open to a quick call to see if there is a fit? If it works, I'll pay you [referral fee] for any clients you send my way."

### Where to Find Partners

- Industry-specific conferences and trade shows
- LinkedIn (search for "[niche] marketing agency [city]")
- Local business networking groups
- Online communities (Skool, industry Slack/Discord groups)
- Google search: "[niche] consultant {{target_city}}"

## Strategy 3: LinkedIn Outreach

Warm outreach via LinkedIn targeting {{target_niche}} business owners and decision-makers. LinkedIn is the most accessible outreach channel for voice AI agencies because business owners are already there in a professional mindset.

### Search Strategy

Find {{target_niche}} owners and managers in {{target_city}}:

- **Search terms:** "{{target_niche}} owner", "{{target_niche}} manager", "practice manager", "office manager"
- **Filters:** Location = {{target_city}}, Industry = relevant industry
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** (optional): More precise filters, InMail credits. Worth it at 20+ leads/month. Manual search is fine for fewer.

### Connection Note Templates

**Value-first approach (recommended):**
> Hi [Name], I work with {{target_niche}} businesses in {{target_city}} on improving how they handle inbound calls and leads. I noticed [something specific about their business from their profile/website]. Would love to connect.

**Direct approach (use sparingly):**
> Hi [Name], I help {{target_niche}} businesses like yours capture revenue from missed calls using AI voice agents. My clients typically see [result metric] within the first month. Open to a quick chat?

### Follow-Up Sequence

| Day | Action | Message Focus |
|-----|--------|---------------|
| Day 0 | Send connection request | Value-first connection note |
| Day 3 | First message (after connection accepted) | Share a relevant insight or statistic about {{target_niche}} |
| Day 7 | Soft pitch | "Would it be helpful if I showed you how [similar business] solved [pain point]?" |
| Day 14 | Case study share | Share a brief case study or result (even if it is a demo result) |
| Day 21 | Final follow-up | "No worries if the timing isn't right. I'll keep sharing useful content here." |

### Content to Share

- Case studies showing before/after call handling metrics
- ROI data specific to {{target_niche}} (e.g., "The average dental practice misses X calls per week")
- Industry-specific insights about voice AI adoption
- Short video demos of your voice agent in action

### Volume Guidelines

- **Starting out:** 5-10 connection requests per day, 100% personalized
- **Getting traction:** 10-20 per day, mix of personalized and semi-templated
- **Scaling:** 20-30 per day with LinkedIn Sales Navigator

Track all LinkedIn interactions with `/agency-ops:outreach`.

## Strategy 4: Content Marketing

Build authority in {{target_niche}} through content that demonstrates your expertise. Content marketing compounds over time -- what you create today generates leads for months.

### Content Types by Effort and Impact

| Content Type | Effort | Impact | Best For |
|-------------|--------|--------|----------|
| LinkedIn posts | Low (15-30 min) | Medium | All stages -- start here |
| Case studies | Medium (1-2 hours) | High | Getting traction+ (need real results) |
| Blog posts/articles | High (2-4 hours) | Medium | Scaling (SEO plays take time) |
| Video/podcast | High (2-4 hours) | High | Scaling (builds strong personal brand) |

### Content Ideas for Voice AI Agencies

**Pain point content:**
- "How {{target_niche}} businesses lose $X/month from missed calls"
- "The hidden cost of slow lead response in {{target_niche}}"
- "Why your receptionist can't keep up (and what to do about it)"

**Results content:**
- "Before/after case study: [client] reduced missed calls by Y%"
- "How [client name] added $Z/month in revenue with an AI receptionist"
- "30 days of data: what happened when [niche] business stopped missing calls"

**Educational content:**
- "The 3 types of voice AI every {{target_niche}} needs"
- "Voice AI vs. answering services: a real cost comparison"
- "What to look for when choosing a voice AI provider for your {{target_niche}}"

### Distribution Channels

- **LinkedIn** (primary): Where your prospects spend professional time
- **Industry forums and communities**: Niche-specific Facebook groups, Reddit, industry forums
- **Skool community**: Share wins and insights with other operators (builds reputation)
- **Email newsletter**: Once you have 50+ contacts, start a simple monthly update

### Recommended Cadence

- **Minimum viable:** 2-3 LinkedIn posts per week
- **Growth mode:** Add 1 case study per month and 1 in-depth piece per quarter
- **Scaling:** Daily LinkedIn presence, weekly newsletter, monthly long-form content

## Strategy 5: Networking and Events

In-person and virtual networking in industry-specific communities. Networking has high conversion rates because people buy from people they know and trust.

### Where to Network

**In-person:**
- {{target_niche}} trade shows and industry conferences
- Local Chamber of Commerce events
- BNI (Business Network International) chapters
- Small business meetups and startup events
- Industry-specific associations (state dental association, local bar association, etc.)

**Virtual:**
- Industry webinars and online masterminds
- Skool communities (both Amplify and niche-specific)
- Discord/Slack communities for {{target_niche}} professionals
- LinkedIn Groups focused on {{target_niche}} business owners

### Elevator Pitch Framework

**Template:** "I help {{target_niche}} businesses [solve pain point] using AI phone agents. My clients typically see [specific result] within [timeframe]."

**Examples by niche:**
- **Dental:** "I help dental practices answer every call, 24/7, using an AI receptionist. My clients capture 30-40% more appointment bookings from calls that used to go to voicemail."
- **HVAC:** "I help HVAC companies respond to emergency service requests in under 60 seconds, day or night, using AI voice agents."
- **Law Firms:** "I help law firms capture every potential client inquiry with an AI intake specialist that qualifies leads and books consultations automatically."

### Follow-Up Protocol

- Connect on LinkedIn within 24 hours of meeting someone
- Send a personalized follow-up message referencing your conversation
- Add them to your outreach tracking via `/agency-ops:outreach`
- If they expressed interest, schedule a follow-up call within the week

## Strategy 6: Cold Email (Scaling)

For operators with 3+ clients and proven results. Higher volume, lower conversion -- but effective at scale when you have strong social proof.

### Prerequisites

Do NOT start cold email until you have:
- 2+ case studies with real, measurable results
- A professional website or landing page
- A clear value proposition refined through earlier strategies
- Enough pipeline capacity to handle responses

### Email Sourcing

- **Apollo.io:** Business email lookup, free tier available
- **Hunter.io:** Find email addresses from company domains
- **Manual research:** Check business websites for contact emails (owner, manager, or general inquiry)

Note: This is email LOOKUP from business websites and directories, not scraping.

### Email Sequence (3 emails over 10 days)

**Email 1 (Day 1) -- Pain point:**
> Subject: Quick question about {{business_name}}'s phone calls
>
> Hi [Name], I noticed [something specific about their business]. I work with {{target_niche}} businesses in {{target_city}} and one thing I hear consistently is that missed calls are costing real revenue.
>
> [Client name] was in a similar situation -- we set up an AI voice agent and they captured [specific result] in the first month.
>
> Worth a 15-minute call to see if this could work for you?

**Email 2 (Day 4) -- Social proof:**
> Subject: Re: Quick question about {{business_name}}'s phone calls
>
> Following up on my note from [day]. Quick case study that might be relevant:
>
> [Brief 2-3 sentence case study with specific metrics]
>
> If timing isn't right, no worries -- just let me know and I won't follow up again.

**Email 3 (Day 10) -- Breakup:**
> Subject: Should I close your file?
>
> Hi [Name], I sent a couple of notes about helping {{business_name}} with [pain point]. I haven't heard back, so I'll assume the timing isn't right.
>
> If things change, feel free to reach out. I'll keep sharing useful content about voice AI in {{target_niche}} on LinkedIn if you want to connect there.

### Compliance

- **CAN-SPAM requirements:** Include your physical address and an unsubscribe option in every email
- **Opt-out honoring:** Remove anyone who unsubscribes or asks to stop immediately
- **Send limits:** Stay under 50 emails/day when starting to protect sender reputation
- **Volume targets:** 50-100 emails per week maximum when ramping up

## Tracking Your Pipeline

Regardless of which strategies you use, track these metrics monthly:

| Metric | What It Tells You | Target |
|--------|-------------------|--------|
| Leads generated | Total new prospects across all channels | Depends on stage |
| Response rate | % of outreach that gets a reply | 15-25% (LinkedIn), 5-10% (email) |
| Discovery calls booked | Conversations with qualified prospects | 5-10/month for getting traction |
| Close rate | % of discovery calls that become clients | 20-30% |
| Cost per lead | Total spend / leads generated | Under $10/lead for most strategies |
| Lead source attribution | Which strategy produced each client | Track in outreach files |

Use `/agency-ops:outreach` to track individual leads and `/agency-ops:pipeline` to manage deals through your sales process.
