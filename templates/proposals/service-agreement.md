Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice to the other party.

### For Cause

Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party:

- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy

### Post-Termination

Upon termination:
1. Client will have **14 days** to export all data, call recordings, and configurations
2. Provider will assist with data export and transition at no additional charge during this period
3. Provider will delete all Client data from its systems within 30 days after the export period
4. Any outstanding invoices remain due and payable
5. If Client has prepaid for unused months, Provider will issue a pro-rated refund
