# Outreach Message Templates

These are starter templates for multi-channel outreach. All `{niche}` placeholders get replaced with your actual niche from `context/agency.md` when displayed via `/agency-ops:outreach templates`. Personalize beyond the template -- these are starting points, not scripts.

## LinkedIn Templates

### Connection Request Note

> Hi {name}, I help {niche} businesses automate their phone handling with voice AI -- fewer missed calls, faster response times. Would love to connect.

*(Keep under 120 characters for LinkedIn's connection note limit.)*

### First Follow-Up Message

> Thanks for connecting, {name}! I noticed {company} is doing great work in the {niche} space.
>
> One thing I've been seeing with {niche} businesses is that missed calls and slow lead response are costing them 20-30% of potential revenue. We've been helping practices like yours automate that with voice AI -- calls get answered instantly, leads get callbacks within 60 seconds.
>
> Would it be worth a quick 15-minute chat to see if that could help {company}?

### Second-Connection Intro Request

> Hi {mutual}, I noticed you're connected with {name} at {company}. I help {niche} businesses automate their phone systems and lead response with voice AI -- it's been a game-changer for practices that are missing calls or losing leads to slow follow-up.
>
> Would you be open to making a quick intro? I think {name} would find it valuable. Happy to return the favor anytime.

### LinkedIn Second-Connection Strategy

**How to find warm leads through mutual connections:**

1. **Search filters:** Use LinkedIn search with Industry ({niche}) + Location (your target area) + Role (owner, manager, director)
2. **Identify mutual connections:** Look for the "2nd" badge and check shared connections
3. **Evaluate fit:** Check their company page -- do they have a phone number listed? Are they the right size (5-50 employees)?
4. **Choose your approach:**
   - If you have a strong mutual connection: Use the Intro Request template above
   - If the mutual connection is weak: Send a direct connection request with your Connection Request Note
   - If no mutual connections: Send a cold connection request (still use the note)
5. **Volume:** Aim for 10-15 connection requests per day (LinkedIn limits to ~100/week)

## Email Templates

### Cold Outreach Email

**Subject:** Quick question about {company}'s phone calls

> Hi {name},
>
> I work with {niche} businesses on a specific problem: missed calls and slow lead follow-up. The average {niche} practice misses 30% of incoming calls -- each one a potential customer walking to a competitor.
>
> We set up voice AI that answers every call instantly, books appointments, and calls back missed leads within 60 seconds. One {niche} client added $12K/month in revenue in the first 90 days.
>
> Would a 15-minute call this week make sense to see if this fits {company}?
>
> Best,
> {your_name}

### Follow-Up Email

**Subject:** Re: Quick question about {company}'s phone calls

> Hi {name},
>
> Just following up on my note from a few days ago. I know {niche} owners are busy -- that's actually exactly the problem we solve.
>
> Here's a quick example: we helped a {niche} practice go from missing 8-10 calls per day to zero missed calls, with automated scheduling and lead callbacks. Their new patient bookings went up 40% in the first month.
>
> Worth a quick chat? I can show you exactly how it works in 15 minutes.
>
> Best,
> {your_name}

### Break-Up Email

**Subject:** Should I close the loop?

> Hi {name},
>
> I've reached out a couple of times about helping {company} with missed calls and lead response. Looks like the timing might not be right, and that's completely fine.
>
> If things change down the road, I'm always happy to chat. In the meantime, here's a quick tip: even setting up a simple missed-call text-back can recover 15-20% of lost leads.
>
> All the best,
> {your_name}

## Phone Script Outline

### Cold Call Script

**Opener:**
> Hi {name}, this is {your_name} with {agency_name}. I help {niche} businesses with a really specific problem -- do you have 30 seconds?

**Pain point question:**
> How are you currently handling calls when your team is busy or after hours? Are you seeing many go to voicemail?

**Value proposition (15 seconds):**
> We set up voice AI that answers every call, books appointments, and calls back missed leads within 60 seconds. {Niche} businesses we work with typically see a 30-40% increase in booked appointments.

**Soft ask:**
> I'd love to show you a quick demo of how it sounds -- could we set up a 15-minute call later this week?

**Handle "not interested":**
> Totally understand. If it helps, I can send over a one-page case study from a {niche} business we worked with -- no commitment at all. Would that be okay?

## Usage Notes

- All templates use `{name}`, `{company}`, `{niche}`, `{mutual}`, `{your_name}`, `{agency_name}` placeholders
- The `/agency-ops:outreach templates` command replaces `{niche}` with your actual niche from `context/agency.md`
- Always personalize beyond the template -- mention something specific about their business
- Follow-up cadence: 3 days, 5 days, 7 days, 14 days (then mark as cold if no response)
- LinkedIn connection request notes must stay under 120 characters
- Email subject lines should be under 50 characters for mobile readability
