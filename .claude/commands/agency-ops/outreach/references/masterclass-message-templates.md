# Sales Masterclass: Message Templates

Source: Amplify Voice AI Sales Masterclass. These are the canonical message templates. Personalize them, but use these as your base.

## Warm Outreach Templates

### 1. Group Chat Message
> "Anyone know any [dentists]? I'm building something for them and would love some early feedback."

Use in: WhatsApp groups, Facebook groups, Slack communities. Replace [dentists] with your niche.

### 2. Intro Request (message for friend to forward)
> "Hey David, my friend is working on something that could help dentists with [reactivating their database] and bring clients back. He'd love some early feedback. So I thought of you. Cool if I connect you?"

Use: Send this to your friend/mutual connection. They forward it to the prospect. Replace names and niche specifics.

### 3. Warm DM (Instagram/WhatsApp/phone/LinkedIn)
> "Hey Sarah, hope you're doing well. Quick question. Do you know anyone who runs a dental practice? I'm working on something that helps them reactivate their dead database and I'm looking for feedback."

Use: Direct message to any warm contact. The "do you know anyone" framing removes pressure. Replace niche and service.

### 4. LinkedIn Intro Request (ask friend for intro to 2nd connection)
> "Hey Sarah, I saw you're connected with David on LinkedIn. Would you be open to introducing us? I'm building something for dentists and would love some early feedback from someone in the space."

Use: When you find a 2nd-degree connection on LinkedIn who fits your ICP.

### 5. LinkedIn 2nd Connection Direct (connection request note)
> "Hey David, how do you know Sarah? We went to college together. Thanks for connecting. Would love to hear about your experience in [dental/roofing/medspa]."

Use: After connecting with a 2nd-degree connection. Reference the mutual connection to build rapport.

## Cold Outreach Templates

### 6. Loom Script (for cold LinkedIn outreach)
> "Hey, thanks for connecting. I actually got a chance to look at your website, and I saw that you have a form on that website. Well, I actually help dentists who have forms on their websites make sure that when they get filled out by a potential patient, we reach out to them immediately. We book an appointment so they don't go to the next dentist over in Google Maps. I've done this for two other people. And they're getting about 10 more patients per month because of the system. Happy to jump on a quick call and show it to you."

Customize: Replace "dentists" with your niche. Replace "form on that website" with whatever specific thing you noticed. Replace results with your own (or say "I'm building this for" if no results yet).

## Usage Notes

- Replace all niche references with your actual niche from `context/agency.md`
- The warm templates use the "feedback" framing on purpose -- it's non-threatening and opens doors
- The Loom script is meant to be recorded as a video, not sent as text
- Over time, your modifications to these templates will be captured in `learnings.md` so Claude can generate "more like you" versions
