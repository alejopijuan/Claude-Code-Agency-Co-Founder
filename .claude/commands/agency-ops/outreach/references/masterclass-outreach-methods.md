# Sales Masterclass: Outreach Methods

Source: Amplify Voice AI Sales Masterclass

## The Outreach Funnel

100 messages → 30 responses (70% won't answer) → 10 conversations → 5 discovery calls → 1-2 clients

The 100-message challenge: Send 100 messages (can space out 20/day for 5 days). Most people stop at 10 and give up. That doesn't count.

## Warm Outreach Sources (5 Places)

### 1. LinkedIn (2nd Connections)
Search with filters: My Network → Connections → Search with Filters → select 2nd connections, filter by industry/location. Look for mutual connections who can introduce you.

### 2. Google Calendar
Export Google Calendar (Settings → Export), give to Claude to extract contacts from meetings. People you've met are warm contacts.

### 3. WhatsApp
Individual contacts + group chats. Post in groups, don't pitch directly. Use the Group Chat Message template.

### 4. Instagram
DM contacts you already know. Use the Warm DM template. Comment on their content first.

### 5. Phone Contacts
Scroll through your phone contacts. You'll be amazed how many people you know who might know someone in your target niche.

## Cold Outreach Methods (5 Methods)

| Method | Difficulty | Conversion | Notes |
|--------|-----------|------------|-------|
| Loom + LinkedIn | 3/5 | 4/5 | Connect on LinkedIn (3rd connections), when they accept send a Loom screen recording of their website showing how you can help. Matt Cohn's method. |
| Instagram DMs | 1/5 | 2/5 | Follow, comment on content first, then DM. Bilal got 30% reply rate on 100 DMs. Don't be pitchy. |
| In-Person/Conferences | 2/5 | 5/5 | Most effective method by far. Just need a recorded demo. Expensive (travel/tickets) but 20-50 conversations per day possible. |
| Paid Ads | 2/5 | 1/5 | Don't do this until you have proof + capital. Copy what works from Meta Ad Library. Lead quality is "abysmal." |
| Cold Calling | 5/5 | 3/5 | Hardest method. Leads are extremely skeptical. Amber King's 5 vocal foundations: Rate, Volume, Pitch, Tonality (smile), Pause. |

### Loom + LinkedIn Strategy (Recommended #1 Cold Method)

1. Find prospects via LinkedIn search (3rd connections in your niche)
2. Send connection request (no pitch in the note)
3. When they accept, record a personalized Loom video walking through their website
4. Send the Loom link as your first message
5. The Loom should show a specific problem you can solve for them

**Loom Script Template:**
"Hey, thanks for connecting. I actually got a chance to look at your website, and I saw that you have a form on that website. Well, I actually help dentists who have forms on their websites make sure that when they get filled out by a potential patient, we reach out to them immediately. We book an appointment so they don't go to the next dentist over in Google Maps. I've done this for two other people. And they're getting about 10 more patients per month because of the system. Happy to jump on a quick call and show it to you."

## Five Dimensions of Your Edge

1. **Distribution** — More important than product in 2026. How are you getting in front of buyers?
2. **CRM Partnerships** — Deep CRM integration can't be undercut by $49/month solutions.
3. **Trust Architecture** — Everything online feels fake. Build trust by default: conferences and CRM marketplace listings.
4. **Knowledge Capture** — Your industry expertise can't be replicated. The more you work with one type of client, the more valuable you become.
5. **Early Market Arbitrage** — The illusion of saturation. Your LinkedIn bubble ≠ the rest of the world. Market growing 15x over next 8 years.
